For Our Community

You've Served. We've Got You.

The Samuel Council Chapter of the 555th PIA is committed to more than preserving history, we're committed to the well-being of the veterans and military families in our community. This page collects the most important local, state, and federal resources available to veterans in the Fayetteville, NC and Fort Liberty area.

If you know of a resource that should be listed here, contact us and we'll get it added.

Know What You've Earned

Key Benefits Overview

01

Disability Compensation

Tax-free monthly payments for veterans with service-connected injuries or conditions. Ratings range from 0%–100%. File or reopen a claim through the VA or a VSO at no cost.

02

GI Bill, Education & Training

Post-9/11 GI Bill covers tuition, housing, and books for college, vocational school, and training programs. Transferable to dependents in some cases. Montgomery GI Bill also available.

03

VA Home Loan Guaranty

Purchase or refinance a home with no down payment required and no private mortgage insurance. One of the most powerful financial benefits available to veterans.

04

VA Healthcare Enrollment

Eligible veterans can enroll in VA healthcare for primary care, mental health, prescriptions, and specialty services. Priority groups determine cost-sharing. Enroll online or at any VA facility.

05

Survivors & Dependents Benefits

Dependency and Indemnity Compensation (DIC), Survivors Pension, and Burial Benefits are available to eligible surviving spouses, children, and parents of deceased veterans.

06

Small Business Resources

The SBA's Boots to Business program and VBOC (Veteran Business Outreach Centers) offer training, mentorship, and loan programs for veteran entrepreneurs.
